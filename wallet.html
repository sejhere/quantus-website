FEATURES

Built for humans.

01

Reversible Transactions

Say goodbye to test transactions.

Set a time window on outgoing transfers. Changed your mind? Cancel before the window closes. A safety net that does not compromise finality.

02

Checkphrases

Never fat finger again.

Every Quantus address has a human-readable checkphrase. A short sequence of memorable words generated from your address. Verify the phrase before you send. Protect against typos, copy-paste tampering, and address poisoning attacks.

03

High-Security Accounts

For institutions and advanced users.

Create a dead-man switch or assign a guardian to your account who can revert malicious transactions. Guardians can cancel transactions during the reversal period, but not spend on your behalf.

Opt-in and composable.
